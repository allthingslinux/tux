# General

## Can I invite Tux to my server?

A publicly hosted Tux is planned. For now you can [self-host Tux](../selfhost/index.md) on your own infrastructure.

## Is Tux free?

Yes. Tux is free and open source under the [GPL-3.0](https://github.com/allthingslinux/tux/blob/main/LICENSE) license. You can use, modify, and contribute to it without cost.

## How can I support Tux?

- **Contribute Code:** Open a [GitHub issue](https://github.com/allthingslinux/tux/issues) or send a pull request.
- **Discuss and Brainstorm:** Jump into our [Discord](https://discord.gg/gpmSjcjQxg) server and reach out.
- **Donate Financially:** Donate to the All Things Linux [Open Collective](https://opencollective.com/allthingslinux).

## Can you add a specific feature?

Open a [GitHub issue](https://github.com/allthingslinux/tux/issues) with the `[FEATURE]` prefix, or send a pull request. We welcome ideas from the community.

## Where can I see the roadmap?

The [roadmap](https://github.com/allthingslinux/tux/issues/525) is tracked in GitHub. It outlines planned features and the path to v0.1.0.
